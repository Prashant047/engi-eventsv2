Date:
Time:
Venue:

Rules

ONE ON ONE POPPING BATTLE 1. The two participants be on opposite sides of the stage.
2. The songs played during the battle would be randomly played by the DJ.
3. There would be two rounds of performances during each battle.
4. There would not be any point system in the battle round. The winner would be announced after each battle by the judge’s discretion.
5. The time given to each contestant in the preliminary rounds would be approx. 30 seconds.
6. In the finals, the time limit for each performer in each round will be approx. 1 minute.
7. The judging criteria for the popping will be based on-
i) ORIGINAL POPPING MOVEMENTS (Foundation, Techniques and Variations of styles ex: Popping, Boogaloo, Tutting, Animation, Snaking etc...)
ii) CHARACTER AND CHARISMA (Eye contact and Interaction with environment)
iii) MUSICALITY (Pure Funk, Improvisation and Interaction with the music)
iv) STYLE AND GRACE (Execution and Demonstration of body control through balance, strength and range of motion)
v) ATTITUDE (No fear Confidence, High energy, Good sportsmanship. Ability to experiment through improvisation and making any mistakes into something, Focus on the music...not moves or worries!)
vi) FLOOR WORK (Within the Popping vocabulary)
vii) STAGE PRESENCE (Entertainment quality/Performance/Audience connection)
viii) CREATIVITY (....Add your flavor, Be Extraordinary)

TWO ON TWO POPPING BATTLE 1. All preliminary rounds would be two rounds each and each performer would get approx. 30 seconds to perform.
2. In the final would, there would be 3 rounds.
3. The time limit for each performer during preliminary rounds would be approx. 30 seconds.
4. The time limit for each performer during the finals would be approx. 1 minute.
5. The music played by the DJ would be random and would feature funk, rap, break beats, electronica etc.
6. The judging would be non-point based and the sole discretion of the judge would decide who wins the round.
7. The result would depend upon the combined performance of both the team members.

FOUR ON FOUR All styles BATTLE 1. The number of members in the crew should be 4.
2. Total time duration for the preliminary rounds would be 6 minutes.
3. The changeover between the crews would be done at a gap of 30 seconds.
4. The finals would feature a total time duration of 8 minutes.
5. Elements of breaking must be incorporated in the performance namely toprock, downrock, freezes and power moves along with other dance forms of hip hop.
6. The judging would not be point based and would be done on the discretion of the judge based on overall team performance.

ONE ON ONE BREAKING BATTLE : 1. The two participants be on opposite sides of the stage.
2. The songs played during the battle would be randomly played by the DJ.
3. There would be two rounds of performances during each battle.
4. There would not be any point system in the battle round. The winner would be announced after each battle by the judge’s discretion.
5. The time given to each contestant in the preliminary rounds would be approx. 30 seconds.
6. In the finals, the time limit for each performer in each round will be approx. 1 minute.

Nukkad

Nayan Lal - 9540669462

Info

Nukkad, the street event, brings out the issues and afflictions that ravage our nation at every moment, each day, and attempts to bring a change in the situations of the afflicted.
Date:
Time:
Venue:

Rules

PRELIMS/ELIMINATIONS:
All the participating teams must mail a short synopsis (50-100 words) of their scripts and the script to pratibimb.dtu@gmail.com 15 teams will be shortlisted on basis of script, past performances (preference will be given to teams registering first)

FINALS:
Team size: No restrictions
Time limit: 22+2 minutes (empty stage to empty stage)
Teams must perform the same street play which was mentioned in the script eliminations.

Judging Criteria:
Acting, Voice (sync, modulation, diction), Screenplay, Script, Crowd interaction and Overall impact.

General Rules:
1. The team size represents the number of people registered as a team. Only these shall be allowed to perform the street play.
2. Minimum of 8 actors are to be included in the team.
3. Music accompanists are included in the team number stated above.
4. Teams are expected to perform at an open air venue, on a circular stage with audience all around.
5. No electrical appliances shall be allowed during the performance, whether inside or outside the circle of performance.
6. Only live music is allowed. Teams will have to bring their own instruments - no instrument shall be provided.
7. Language of the performance should be Hindi/English or both. However short sub passages in other languages are allowed.
8. Any kind of fluid, live animals, flame, heavy object or any material which has a possibility of damaging the stage is not allowed.
9. Dry colours may be used during the play, but the teams have to clean the stage after their performance and the cleaning time will be included in the performance time.
10. Teams will be heavily penalised if they exceed the time limit.
11. Plays can be based on any theme but since performance is being held at public venues, any content directed against a certain political group or religious community is strictly prohibited.
12. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind is not allowed and may lead to disqualification.
13. Any kind of plagiarism and profanity will lead to immediate disqualification.
14. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
